About Hanu Tech

Hanu Tech is a trusted facade fabrication and installation company specializing in HPL Cladding, Fundermax Panels, ACP Cladding, Glass Facades, Louvers, and Architectural Exterior Solutions. We combine technical expertise, quality materials, and precision workmanship to create durable and visually stunning building exteriors.
From concept to completion, we work closely with architects, developers, and contractors to deliver innovative facade solutions that enhance both the appearance and performance of modern buildings.
